Why it exists

Course choice should not depend on who you happen to know.

Less guesswork

Handbook entries and official summaries matter, but they rarely answer the questions students ask each other: workload, assessment clarity, teaching style, and whether the course matches the pitch.

Better decisions

ANRevU is designed to reduce information asymmetry by collecting practical student experience in one place, helping students compare courses before they commit.

Student helping student

The platform only works if students contribute honestly and constructively. Every review helps the next student make a more informed choice.

Independent and not-for-profit

ANRevU is student-funded and not-for-profit. The goal is useful academic transparency, not selling student attention or pretending to speak for the university.

Built for the moment before enrolment, when a clear student perspective can change a whole semester.
